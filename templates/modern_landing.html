Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Tajir POS

What is Tajir POS?

It's a simple billing and accounting system on mobile/tablet that helps shops issue invoices, track sales, and follow UAE rules.

How much does it cost?

It's made affordable for small shops. Much cheaper than other systems, and no hidden charges. We offer a free trial so you can try it first.

Why do I need this? I'm fine with paper bills.

From July 2026, the UAE government will not allow handwritten bills. Only e-invoices from approved systems will be legal. Tajir POS is already ready for this requirement.

I don't have VAT, do I still need it?

Yes, even if you are not VAT registered, the law applies to all businesses. You will still need to issue digital invoices.

If I have a problem, who will help me?

We provide full support on WhatsApp and phone in English, Arabic, and Hindi/Urdu. You won't be left alone.

Is it complicated to use?

Not at all. It's designed for small shops, very simple to use. If you know WhatsApp, you can use Tajir POS.

But the law starts next year in July, why should I spend money now?

That's true, but if you wait until the last minute, it will be expensive and stressful — every shop will rush to change at the same time. By starting now, you learn the system slowly and get comfortable before it becomes mandatory. Also, Tajir POS helps you today to track sales, control stock, and avoid mistakes. So you get benefits right away, not just in 2026.
